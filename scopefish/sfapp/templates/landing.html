How it works

Three steps from raw publications to your personalized digest.

1

Fetch

Every week we query OpenAlex, Semantic Scholar, and CrossRef for papers matching IGB's top 100+ research concepts.

2

Rank

Each paper is scored by semantic similarity to IGB's research profile, concept overlap, citation proximity, and recency.

3

Discover

Papers are organized into your weekly digest with relevance explanations, department tags, and direct links to full text.
